WHO WE ARE

We're a small team called the Scam Protectors that consists of four individuals from the University of Utah.

WHAT WE DO

We help prevent loved ones and vulnerable technology users (VTUs) from becoming victims of social engineering attempts through Gmail.

HOW WE DO IT

We utilize natural language processing and machine learning to scrub emails of sensitive personal information (i.e. credit/debit card numbers, social security numbers) in order to prevent hostile email senders from scamming VTUs. Once you sign up with a valid email, you can authenticate additional email addresses (including the one used to sign up) to have monitored for social engineering attempts.
